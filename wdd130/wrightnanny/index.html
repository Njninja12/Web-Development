Home Nanny Cleaning

Are you too busy to keep your house clean and organized? Or have you been wanting a free night and haven't gotten one for months. Well sometimes you just got to find the Wright nanny. My name is Hannah Wright and I started this business to help those families who can't seem to find the time to go to dinner or have a date night because they can't get a nanny or the nanny is too expensive. Well I'm here to help relive some of that burden by offering my services of good quality at affordable prices.

Hey everyone, I'm hannah Wright. I'm currently going into my senior year at Westwood High School. I have been nannying for close to 3 years now and have loved every second of it. I started out with my cousins and my neighbors kids, but soon they started spreading the word and now I have become a successful business. I realized when a couple families tried to overpay me that I noticed that nannies can be expensive. So that is why I charge an affordable price so families that can't afford expensive agency can rely on a good quality nanny to help them out.

I wanted to be a symbol to other people that nanny's don't have to be over the top outrageous prices to be good quality. Nanny should be about tkaing car of the children and providing care so that the parents may have a lighter load to carry.

I started a cleaning service once I realized that I like to clean while I babysit. So for the families that have older kids or for times when you don't need a nanny, but don't have the time to clean up or organize, That is why a I started this second servcie.
