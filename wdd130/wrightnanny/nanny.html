Home Nanny Cleaning

Over the years I have been nannying I have done many things to help ease the burden of parents who are looking for a night out free of worry. some of the manythings include, feeding, playing, bathing and getting them tucked into bed so that you can come home to a nice and quiet house. And almost always I love to do a bit a tidying up while I'm watching the kids so don't be alarmed if you get the bonus of have a cleaner house than when you left, free of charge.

This is Carson. I've lived down the street from his family for years, so i was overjoyed when Carson's parents asked me to come watch Carson for a couple hours one night. I got to know carson so much more when i babysat him that night and had an absolute blast playiong games with him. He was a little sad when his parents got back and I had to leave, but I was super excited when his parents asked if I could watch him evey week so they could have a date night. Getting to hang out with every week has brought an incredible joy into my life and has been an amazing help to his parents as well.

This is my cousin Graysen. I've helped nanny him for going on 2 years now. He is 5 years old and is always excited when he finds out i get to come hang out with him for the evening. His favorite show is Spidey and Friends and he loves it when I take turns with his trains. My aunt never has to worry about her son going with out food or entertainment because i will make them food, play with them and i'll even get them into bed, so their parents can enjoy a night out to themselves.
