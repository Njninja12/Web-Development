Content

Home page

This will be contain images and a biography of Hannah Wright, the owner of this business. The biography will talk about her life and what she loves to do. It will also inlcude how she got into nannying and how she wants to help those families that are too busy to call up an agency and have interviews for a nanny that wouldn't be affordable. This page will also show photos of Hannah with various children she has nannied as well as houses she has helped clean and organize.

Images for the Home page

Nanny

This page will contain information on the nannying side of Hannah's business. It will contain plenty of photos of Hannah with kids she has nannied. It will have information on what she charges per kid as well as what other kinds of services she provides whilst nannying. She can do many other services such as feeding them, helping with schoolwork, putting them to bed.

Images for the Page 2

Cleaning

This page will contain information pertaining to the cleaning services hannah offers. Along wiht nannying she likes to help clean around the house even when she wasn't expected to clean. She has always had that kind gesture when she nannies, so she thought of the wonderful idea of offering it as a stand-alone service as well. This will allow families that have kids that wmay be older and don't need to be babysat a chance to help their busy lifestyles and have someone come and clean and/ or organize. This service doesn't have to apply to families and families alone. She has gone and helped out couples who don't have kids and even couples that don't have kids living at home anymore.
